About Me


I'm a highly skilled Python web developer currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology (BTech) degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the Institute of Engineering and Management. I completed my Higher Secondary (HS) education from Kapsit High School and my Madhyamik education from Kamarpukur Ramakrishna Mission Multipurpose School. With a passion for building efficient, scalable applications and solving complex problems, I have acquired substantial experience in machine learning, deep learning, and generative AI technologies.
I possess strong programming skills in C, C++, Java, Kotlin, and a deep proficiency in Python. My expertise extends to several powerful Python web frameworks such as Flask and Django, which I leverage to create robust, feature-rich web applications.
I am proficient in developing RESTful APIs using Python, ensuring seamless communication between the front-end and back-end components of web applications. I have experience in designing API architectures, implementing authentication and authorization mechanisms, and ensuring efficient data transfer using JSON or XML.
Additionally, I have extensive knowledge in deploying web applications, employing various deployment methodologies and tools to ensure reliable and efficient deployment processes. I am skilled in using containerization technologies like Docker, enabling me to package applications with their dependencies and deploy them across different environments. Furthermore, I have experience with cloud platforms such as Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Google Cloud Platform (GCP) to manage scalable infrastructure and deploy applications efficiently.
Database management is another area where I excel. I have worked with both relational databases, such as MySQL and PostgreSQL, and NoSQL databases like MongoDB. I am adept at designing and optimizing database schemas, writing efficient queries, and ensuring data security and integrity.
Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have collaborated with multidisciplinary teams, including designers, front-end developers, and project managers. I possess excellent communication skills and have consistently translated business requirements into effective technical solutions, ensuring the smooth delivery of projects.
Continuously staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in web development and AI, I am always eager to learn and adopt new technologies and frameworks. Additionally, I have a solid understanding of front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which allows me to collaborate effectively with front-end developers to create intuitive user interfaces.
My strong foundation in machine learning, deep learning, data science, data analytics, advanced computer vision, natural language processing, and generative AI sets me apart as a versatile and innovative developer, capable of tackling diverse challenges in the tech industry.
